About

As a lead-up to NYC's Reinvent Payphone Design Challenge , we are hosting Payphone Hack Day to build physical or virtual payphone prototypes. Designers, engineers, students -- bring yourself and your gear:

  • Form a team and start hacking
  • Meet thought leaders in design, making and open data
  • Get prizes and schwag from our awesome partners

The rules that apply to the City's Reinvent Payphones Design Challenge also apply here.

Questions? Contact George (george.agudow@nyu.edu) or Janine (janine@tokbox.com or @janineyoong).
